What is Solitaire Chess?

An online chess game based on the long running Chess solitaire articles in Chess Life by Bruce Pandolfini. Fundamentally this is a make the next move type of game. Bruce is a well-regarded chess coach. After an inital opening you play one side with each move being scored, I like the commentary and rationale that Bruce provides on why the moves were actually made. I will add more games as I go back through previous issues and as time allows.

A note to beginners

If you are looking more interactive games or are just beginning your chess journey Chess.com and Chessarena.com provide excellent resources.

How to play the game

You can drag or click to move pieces. Your move will then be compared to the actual move made by the GM. Matching the actual move or playing a strong alternative will score points. Your total score will be rated at the end of the game.
